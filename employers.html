Build a Qualified Healthcare Workforce

The Employment and Job Placement Center partners with healthcare employers across New York to support their recruitment needs by matching them with qualified candidates

Why Partner with EJP

As part of EJP's partner network, healthcare employers receive dedicated recruitment support and direct access to a pipeline of recent graduates ready to fill open roles.

Over 7,000 CPT Participants

Access a pipeline of recent graduates trained across a variety of healthcare occupations — from nursing and allied health to technical and administrative roles.

Pre-Screened & Prepared

Our candidates have completed training, received career counseling, and are ready to work. No cold applicants — motivated professionals.

Ongoing Partnership

EJP isn't a one-time service. We build lasting relationships with employers to support continuous staffing and workforce development needs.
