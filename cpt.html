What to Expect

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between MyTEF and iCIMS?

MyTEF is your portal to access career services while iCIMS is your portal to access jobs and see what’s available.

How do I contact CPT staff with a question?

Use the Case Comments section in MyTEF to reach CPT staff directly. You can ask about eligibility, document submissions, or your account status.

Can I access in-person help and guidance?

Yes, the Digital Drop-In Lab is a free resource where you can get hands-on support with your job search, resume, and career services tools.
