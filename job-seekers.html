Why 1199SEIU

More Than Just a Job

An agency might offer higher starting pay — but 1199SEIU offers a career. When you work at an 1199 facility, you're part of the largest healthcare union in the country, with benefits that go far beyond a paycheck.

Job Stability

No sub-contracting clauses protect your position. Fair disciplinary procedures require just cause and union representation. If layoffs happen, you get rehiring rights and transfer options.

Livable Wages

Guaranteed yearly raises, step increases based on experience, improved shift differentials for evenings, nights, weekends, and holidays. Premium pay for certifications.

Career Ladders

Access no-cost education programs including college classes, tuition reimbursement, continuing education units, computer literacy, and technical skills training. Paid time off for classes.

Worker Protections

Low-cost or no-cost health insurance for you and your family — dental, vision, and prescriptions included. Retirement plans with significant employer contributions. A union that fights for adequate staffing and safe working conditions.
