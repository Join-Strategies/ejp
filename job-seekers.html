Why join 1199SEIU

The Power of an 1199 Career

At over 450,000 members strong, 1199ers negotiate together for strong contracts that guarantee fair pay, good benefits, and educational opportunities while leading the fight to support workers across America.

How to Join →

Livable Wages

Members of 1199 have won some of the very best compensation in the country, including guaranteed annual raises, better on-call pay, and more.

Career Ladders

Eligible 1199SEIU members have access to training and education funds to advance their careers, earn new credentials, or maintain the certifications and licenses they already hold.

Worker Protections

1199 members have won workplace protections like banning sub-contracting, fairer disciplinary processes, specific worker-to-patient ratios, and retirement security while leveraging our collective strength to avoid layoffs.

Healthcare Benefits

Eligible 1199 members, both full and part-time, have access to low-cost or no-cost health insurance for themselves and their families.
