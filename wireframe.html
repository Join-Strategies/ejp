About EJP

Mission & Vision

EJP is your long-term partner in healthcare employment. We support job seekers with career services, preparation, and guidance — and connect employers with motivated, qualified candidates from our network.

Our Approach

We believe in collaboration, empowerment, innovation, and impact. Every service we offer is designed to help job seekers prepare, grow, and thrive in New York's healthcare workforce.

The Candidates You Need

Every year, thousands of New Yorkers choose healthcare not because it's a job, but because it's a calling. EJP connects you with qualified, CPT-trained candidates who are motivated and ready to fill your open roles.

Why Partner with EJP

We work with healthcare employers across New York to understand their specific staffing needs and match them with qualified candidates from our network.

🎓

CPT-Trained Candidates

Access a pipeline of graduates trained across 10 healthcare occupations — from nursing and allied health to technical and administrative roles.

✅

Pre-Screened & Prepared

Our candidates have completed training, received career counseling, and are ready to work. No cold applicants — motivated professionals.

🤝

Ongoing Partnership

EJP isn't a one-time service. We build lasting relationships with employers to support continuous staffing and workforce development needs.
